Pantai Ancol

Pantai Ancol is a coastal tourist route consisting of five beaches: Pantai Indah, Elok, Ria, Ancol Festival, and Ancol Carnival. This tourist spot is open 24 hours, offering beautiful panoramas, places to eat, and an ideal location to see sunrise or sunset. Visitors often take pictures, jog, and eat at one of the restaurants in the area. A place to go if you want to populate your Instagram album.

Pantai Ancol is clean enough and has a complete set of tourist facilities. The area is adjacent to various entertainment venues, such as Seaworld and Fantasy World. Pantai Ancol is easily accessible using means of public transportation. For example, by using the Ancol route of Trans Jakarta.

Ocean Dream Samudra

Ocean Dream Samudra, also known as Gelanggang Samudra, is a tourist spot in Jakarta with a sea theme, located in the Taman Impian Jaya Ancol area. Gelanggang Samudra offers sea animal attractions such as dolphins and sea lions, Bird's Paradise Park, from thrilling shows such as Scorpion Rides, to rides such as Jellyfish and Boto-Boto.

Ocean Dream Samudra has complete tourist facilities, including several culinary centers, places of worship, toilets, and clinics. Drinks and ice cream stalls are readily available at several points in the area. Fairly wide walking paths populated with greeneries will provide comfort for families who are enjoying a holiday at this one tourist spot in Jakarta.

Dunia Fantasi

Dunia Fantasi or Dufan is an amusement park in the Taman Impian Jaya Ancol area. This park offers a variety of rides, from thrilling ones like Tornado, Halilintar, Hysteria, to rides suitable for all ages such as Rumah Miring, Poci-Poci, Turangga Rangga, and Pontang-Pontang.

This tourist spot also has themed rides, for example, Ice Age Arctic Adventure, Treasureland, Dufan Glow, Fantasy Lights: Magic of Dufan, and Hello Kitty Adventure. The sound, light, and performance effects of animatronic robots and actors will make these rides even more lively. You can order tickets online to avoid queues.
