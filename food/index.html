Kerak Telor

Kerak telor is a dish made of white glutinous rice, chicken eggs or duck eggs, dried shrimp, fried onions, and topped with fine spices from a mixture of roasted coconut, red chilies, salt, sugar, pepper, ginger and kencur. The egg crust is cooked in a small skillet without using oil.

The half-cooked egg will be turned outside down with the pan. The unique texture of kerak telor and the taste of the delicious kerak telor along with the sticky texture of glutinous rice will long for more of this unique experience.
